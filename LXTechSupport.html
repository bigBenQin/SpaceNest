Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How to get started?

After launching the app, click the "Scan" button and the system will automatically analyze your photos and videos.

Q: What files can be cleaned?

You can clean duplicate photos, similar photos, large photos, large videos, blurry photos, screenshots, live photos, screen recordings, etc.

Q: Does it have video compression feature?

Yes, you can compress video files to save storage space.

Q: Is it safe to delete photos?

You can preview all photos and videos to be deleted before deletion.

  2. Close other apps

Video Compression Failed

  1. Check if storage space is sufficient
  2. Confirm video format is supported
  3. Try compressing again
